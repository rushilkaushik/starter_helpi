Basic Career Assessment:
The Basic Career Assessment offers a streamlined evaluation designed to explore individual strengths, preferences, and aspirations in depth.
It includes fundamental questions about interests, skills, and values, aiming to offer general guidance to individuals exploring their career options. 
This assessment is ideal for those seeking a preliminary understanding of their career preferences and direction.

Detailed Career Assessment:
The Detailed Career Assessment provides a comprehensive analysis tailored to explore individual strengths, preferences, and aspirations in depth.
It encompasses a wide range of questions, covering various aspects such as personality traits, work values, aptitudes, and career goals.
This assessment offers nuanced insights and personalized recommendations, making it suitable for individuals seeking thorough guidance and clarity in their career decision-making process.
